## How I think about product

- The hardest part is never the feature. It's the mental model you're asking someone to adopt.
- Good requirements are falsifiable. "Users can manage access" is a wish. "A workspace admin can grant read-only access to a dataset without contacting engineering" is a requirement.
- The closer you are to the engineering surface, the better your tradeoffs get.
